Pambazo Sandwich

Description

A torta, or sandwich, with a paticular, unique mix of chorizo and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chorizos(3oz. each)
  • 2 1/2 cups potatoes
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Mexican cream

Directions

  1. Cook the chorizo
  2. Add the potatoes as the chorizo is cooking
  3. Add it to the bread
  4. Add cream and lettuce to taste.

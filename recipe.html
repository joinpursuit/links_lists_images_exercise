Bacon Mac & Cheese

Description

Crispy bacon through out mouth watering cheddar cheesy elbow pasta

Ingredients

  • Elbow pasta
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter (optinal)

Directions

  1. Start by boiling 6 cups of water and add pasta. Leave it to cook for 7 to 10 minutes or until your desired texture.
  2. Heat a medium sauce pan and add the bacon. Cook until toasted. once done put on a plate lined with paper towels to drain out any excess grease.
  3. Drain out the pasta and put to the side. Using the same pan you used for cooking the pasta. Bring milk and a table spoon of butter to a low boil and start adding cheese (alot of it).
  4. Once the cheese is thick and gooey, add the pasta and chopped up bacon. Mix well and serve.

The Most Amazing Tehini Sauce

Description

This mouth watering sauce is nutritious and creamy, sour and savoury. Goes great with falafel and fresh veggies inside your pita. Make it with love!

Ingredients

  • Sesame Paste
  • Water
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Optional: Fresh parsley, olive oil, paprika

Directions

  1. Pour your desired amount of sesame paste into a bowl.
  2. Add as much water as the sesame paste.
  3. Mix with a spoon using a stable clockwise motion, until the paste becomes lighter.
  4. Add more water to gain desired texture.
  5. Add a squeeze of lemon juice and salt to taste.
  6. Final mix of all ingredients. Optional: Decorate with fresh parsley on top, a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of paprika.

Prepping

    Materials

  • Mixing glass
  • Bar spoon
  • Rocks glass or coupe glass
  • Iced cubes
  • 1 One inch ice cube
  • Citrus peeler
  • 1 orange
  • Hawthorne strainer
    • -------------------

    Ingredients

  • 1 oz. Gin
  • 1 oz Apertif
  • 1 oz. Sweet Vermouth

Directions

  1. Combine all the ingredients into a mixing glass with iced cubes
  2. Using a bar spoon stir 30-40 rotations in each direction
  3. Use the strainer and place on top lof mixing glass. Strain into your preferred glass
  4. Add the one inch iced cube
  5. To garnish, use the citrus peeler to slice a piece of orange skin.
  6. Rub the orange skin around the rim of the glass
  7. Use the lighter and with the skin facing downwards towards the cocktail, pinch it releasing the oils from the skin. As you do so have the lighter ignited under the skin so that it will create a fiery spritz on to the cocktail surface.
  8. Imbibe,reset,repeat until your face down on the floor

Hot Chocolate Cookies Baby

Description

Chocolate cookie, with a chocolate pistole in the center. Topped with a marshmallow, served warm

Ingredients

  • 10oz butter
  • 1#5oz brown sugar
  • 7oz sugar
  • 8 eggs
  • .25oz coffee extract
  • .5oz vanilla extract
  • 2# Guittard 58% chocolate
  • 1#4oz all purpose flour
  • 3.25oz cocoa powder
  • .75oz baking powder
  • .15oz salt
  1. Combine butter and sugars, but do not cream.
  2. Add eggs, coffee, and vanilla extracts gradually.
  3. Add chocolate and combine.
  4. Sift together all the dry ingredients, and add until just combined.
  5. Use a #50 scoop to scoop onto a pan and place into freezer.
  6. Preheat oven to 325-350 Fahrenheit(depending on oven)
  7. Straight from frozen place frozen dough onto baking sheet, well spaced
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes, then take out to rotate and place chocolate pistole in the center, with marshmallow on top.

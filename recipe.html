AMAZING LASAGNA WITH SOME VEGAN VIBES


Description

This Is The Recipe for Extremely Creamy Partial Vegan Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Gluten Free Lasagna
  • Ground Turkey
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Vegan Cheese
  • Veggies
  • Garlic

Direction

  1. Boil lasagna pasta until soft
  2. Cook the ground tukey thoroughly
  3. Heat tomato sauce
    • Layer first bed of lasagna pasta
    • Layer turkey meat
    • layer tomato sauce
    • layer vegan cheese
    • ***REPEAT FIRST 4 LAYERING STEPS***

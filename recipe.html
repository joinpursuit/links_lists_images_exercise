Hot Dog Biscuits

Hot doggie biscuits for hungry humans!

Ingredients:

  • Easy Bake Biscuits
  • Hot Dogs
  • Black Olives
  • Relish

Directions:

  1. Slice the biscuit dough at angles on both sides to make the ears and slice inbetween half way through to make room for the hot dog.
  2. Bake the biscuits and remove 10 minutes earlier than the recommended cook time.
  3. Insert hot dog in the mouth and decorate with 3 black olives for the eyes and nose and put back in the oven to finish baking.
  4. Decorate with relish and condiments of your choice.
  5. Serve & enjoy!

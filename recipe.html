The Best Bowl of Cereal

Description

Can you cook a fancy meal? Of course you can! But who are you trying to impress? Not a soul. And that's why nothing beats the classic bowl of cereal.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cereal
  • Cold Milk (or non-dairy substitute)
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Directions

  1. Make sure your bowl and spoon are clean, as you'll be eating with them.
  2. Once clean, pour your preferred amount of cereal into your bowl.
  3. Add desired amount of milk to your bowl of cereal.
  4. Enjoy!

Note to cooks

There's some debate as to the listed order of Steps 3. & 4., and really there shouldn't be.
Only godless savages add their milk (or non-dairy substitute) first.
Should this describe you, kindly navigate away from this page IMMEDIATELY!

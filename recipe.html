The Perfect T-ribs recipe

Despcription

The T-ribs are my favorite food of all time. It is easy to prepare and commonly found at prehistoric sites

Ingredients

  • T-rex ribs
  • Chilli powder
  • Garlic powder
  • BBQ sauce
  • Fresh oregano

Directions

  1. Trim off any extra fat from the ribs
  2. Mix all the seasoning together
  3. Season the ribs lightly on the bone side.
  4. Season the ribs more heavily on the meaty side.
  5. Cook throughly until ready

Enjoy!

The Perfect PB & J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my fav.

Ingredients

  • two slices of white bread
  • one jar of jelly
  • one jar of peanut butter
  • a butter knife
  • a sharp knife
  • a cutting board

Description

  1. Lay two slices of bread on each cutting board.
  2. Using the butter knife, spread 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two slices together so that jelly is on top.
  5. Using the sharp knife, cut the sandwich in half

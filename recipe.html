The Perfect PB&J

Description:

This peanutbutter and jelly sandwich is the best sandwich in the whole world. Melty perfection.

Ingredients & Materials

  • Strawberry Jelly
  • Chunky Peanut Butter
  • Two slices of Wheat Bread
  • Butter Knife
  • Toaster
  • Cutting Board

Directions

  1. Toast two slices of wheat bread.
  2. Apply peanut butter to one slice.
  3. Apply jelly to the other slice.
  4. Put together the two slices.
  5. Cut sandwich in half if desired.
  6. Serve with a cold glass of almond milk.

The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 1 spoonful of grape jelly
  • 1 spoonful of creamy Jiff peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread net to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the right side.
  3. On the left side, spread 1/8 inch of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Eat up!

Vegan Creamed Kale

This savory, mouth-watering, non-dairy, creamed kale is the perfect addition for a homecooked winter meal.
This recipe was inspired by a recipe from Diana's Vegan Kitchen

Ingredients (Serves 8-10)

  • 32 oz. of frozen chopped kale
  • 2 cups of unroasted, unsalted cashews
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 medium onions (diced)
  • 2 cloves of garlice (minced)
  • 1/8 tsp fresh grated nutmeg
  • sea salt to taste
  • 2 cups of almond milk
  • 1/2 cup of nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp. of yellow miso powder
  • 4 tbs. of olive oil

Directions

  1. Add onion, garlic, vegetable broth, olive oil and a dash of sea salt to a saucepan. Let simmer until onions are tender stirring occasionally.

  2. In a food processor or blender, mix cashews and almond milk until liquified.

  3. Add frozen kale and cashew mix and remaining ingredietns to saucepan along with the onions.

  4. Cook on low heat until kale is tender. Stir often.

  5. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh grated nutmeg and nutritional yeast.
